Education

"Our Department of Education is dedicated to preparing future educators and academic leaders with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to inspire lifelong learning. The program emphasizes educational theory, classroom management, and modern teaching methodologies to help students become confident, effective, and compassionate teachers. Through a blend of practical training and academic excellence, we aim to shape professionals who contribute meaningfully to the advancement of education and society."

BBA

"Our BBA program builds strong leadership, management, and entrepreneurial skills. It prepares students to excel in the corporate world through practical business knowledge, strategic thinking, and real-world problem-solving."

ADA

"Our ADA program focuses on developing creative, analytical, and communication skills through the study of humanities and social sciences. It prepares students for higher education and a wide range of professional opportunities in arts and related fields."

ADS

"Our ADS program offers a strong foundation in scientific principles and analytical thinking. It prepares students for advanced studies in science and technology while opening pathways to diverse professional careers."

B.Com

"Our B.Com program provides a solid foundation in accounting, finance, economics, and business management. It prepares students for successful careers in commerce, banking, and entrepreneurship with strong analytical and professional skills."

B.S Eng

"Our BS English program develops strong communication, critical thinking, and analytical skills through the study of language, literature, and linguistics. It prepares students for diverse careers in education, media, writing, and research."
